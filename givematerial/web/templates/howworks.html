Language Recommendation

GiveMaterial has a collection of pre-analyzed material (e.g. texts or songs) that it tries to match to your current knowledge of a foreign language. The comparison is usually based on vocabulary, but it can also be based on other information like for example kanji for Japanese.

You can mark texts as read in order to hide them from future recommendations.

External SRS Systems

GiveMaterial does not manage your learning progress. Your learning progress is fetched from other learning tools, for example SRS systems like Anki or Wanikani. You learn your vocabulary and kanji in the external program and on your next visit to GiveMaterial you will see new recommendations.

As for Wanikani, GiveMaterial can automatically pull your learning progress from their programming interface.

Anki does not allow public access to your learning progress (not even through Ankiweb). Thus, for Anki you have to regularly push your updates. This can be achieved with a script from the GiveMaterial repository.
